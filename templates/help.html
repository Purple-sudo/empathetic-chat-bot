Need Help Now?

If you are thinking about hurting yourself or feel in immediate danger, please reach out for help immediately.

If you are in immediate danger

Contact your local emergency number right away.

Examples: 911 (US/Canada), 999 or 112 (UK/EU), or the emergency number in your country.

United States & Canada

Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988, or chat via 988lifeline.org (24/7, free and confidential).

United Kingdom & Ireland

Samaritans: Call 116 123 or visit samaritans.org (24/7).

Australia

Lifeline: Call 13 11 14 or visit lifeline.org.au (24/7).

Other countries

Visit findahelpline.com or search online for your local suicide or crisis hotline.

Aria is an AI chatbot designed for emotional support and de-escalation. It is not a replacement for professional mental health care, and it cannot respond to emergencies. If you are in crisis, please contact a trusted person, a professional, or a crisis service immediately.
