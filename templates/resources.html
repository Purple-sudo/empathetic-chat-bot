Therapy / professional support
  • Consider contacting a licensed therapist, counselor, or your primary care doctor.
  • If you have access to employee/student assistance programs (EAP), those can be a good starting point.
  • If you are in a crisis: go to the urgent help page.
Self-care and coping
  • Grounding: notice 5 things you can see, 4 you can feel, 3 you can hear, 2 you can smell, 1 you can taste.
  • Breathing: inhale 4 seconds, hold 2, exhale 6. Repeat for 2 minutes.
  • Support: message or call someone you trust and tell them what you need (listening, company, help making a plan).
